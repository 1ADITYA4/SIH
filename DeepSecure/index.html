Understanding Deep Fakes

Deep fakes are synthetic media in which a person in an existing image or video is replaced with someone else's likeness. Powered by AI technologies, such as deep learning and generative adversarial networks (GANs), deep fakes can be highly convincing and are being used in a variety of contexts, from entertainment to malicious activities.

The impact of deep fakes on society is profound. They can be used to spread misinformation, create non-consensual explicit content, defame individuals, and manipulate political events. Detecting deep fakes is crucial to maintain trust and authenticity in digital media.
